Data Protection Compliance Statement

Last Update: 03/01/2025

Closure Next is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of its users. We prioritize transparency and ensure that all personal data rights are respected in accordance with international data protection regulations.

No Data Collection:

Closure Next does not collect any personal data from its users. Since the project is open-source, the code is fully transparent, and anyone can inspect it to verify that no data is being collected, processed, or stored. This includes any direct interactions or usage of the platform—no personal information is gathered through the project itself.

Third-Party Platforms:

While Closure Next may use third-party platforms or services for certain functionalities (such as hosting, communication, or social media integrations), responsibility for user data lies with those third-party platforms. Users are free to choose whether or not to interact with these platforms, and any data provided to them is managed by the platforms themselves. Closure Next cannot be held responsible for how these third-party platforms handle personal data. Users engaging with these platforms do so at their discretion and must review the data privacy policies of the respective services they use.

Legal Compliance:

Closure Next aligns with the following data protection regulations:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – European Union

- Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) – Brazil

- California Consumer Privacy Act / California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA) – California, USA

- Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) – Canada

- Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) – Japan

- Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) – Hong Kong

- Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) – Singapore

- Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) – Australia

- Law on Personal Data Protection (LPDP) - Russian

- Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) - United Arab Emirates (UAE)

- Personal Data Protection Law (LPDP) - Mexico

- Data Protection Act (DPA) - India

- Data Protection Act (DPA) - South Africa

- Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) - Malaysia

- Personal Data Protection Law (LDPD) - Argentina

- Personal Data Privacy Bill (PDPB) - Philippines

- Data Privacy Act (DPA) - Philippines

- Personal Data Protection Act (PDP) - Taiwan

- Data Protection Law (Loi Informatique et Libertés) - France

- Data Protection Act (PDA) - United Kingdom (UK)

By not collecting any personal data and by being fully transparent through open-source code, Closure Next ensures that it remains in full compliance with these laws and offers users full control over their personal data.

For any concerns regarding data privacy or to report a potential security issue, please contact us at security@milesonerd.8shield.net.
